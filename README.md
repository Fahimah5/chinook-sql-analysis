# Chinook SQL Analysis

## Overview
SQL data analysis on the Chinook music store database answering 
real business questions using Python and SQLite.

## Tools Used
- Python
- SQL (SQLite)
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
Chinook Database — a sample music store database with 11 tables 
including customers, invoices, tracks, artists and genres.

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which country has the most customers?
2. Which country generates the most revenue?
3. Who are the top 5 customers by total spending?
4. What are the top 10 most popular music genres?
5. What are the top 10 best selling artists?

## Key Findings
1. USA has the most customers (13) and highest revenue ($523.06)
2. Helena Holý is the top spending customer at $49.62
3. Rock is the most popular genre with 835 purchases
4. Iron Maiden is the best selling artist with 140 sales
5. Canada is the second largest market after USA
